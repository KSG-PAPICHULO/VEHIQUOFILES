The Vehiquo Story

Vehicle Enthusiast

VehiQuo is a web and mobile-based platform. It is A Timeline based on vehicle health, service, records, and traffic violation monitoring system. VehiQuo will keep your vehicle records for easy access to any third party auto shops. With the use of VehiQuo, the car owner can easily go to any auto shops and have their records pulled up to see what is due for maintenance like cleaning the brakes, changing oil, have your engine tuned up and many more. The system also provides personal and shared access for browsing through a timeline format of records according to weeks, months or year. VehiQuo will also record the traffic violation made by the owner that has to do with the vehicles. One example is a broken plate number. It will also help you remember your mandatory regular vehicle maintenance through SMS notification. VehiQuo is an application that can help and consumes less time for your auto maintenance and recordkeeping needs.

Our Founding

VEHIQUO was founded by a group of friends who wanted to make a changed in the Auto Industry. These young individuals wanted to leave a mark in the world that would ease maintaining & repairing their beloved vehicles, making it super convenient for them. They aim to make a long lasting relationship with their future partners & clients.
